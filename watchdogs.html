WatchDogs

With the advancement of Ai technology in the recent years, online scams and fraudulent are now are more intrusive than ever. Watchdogs is a tool that detects and reports scams, misinformantion, and potentially Ai generated content, by using GPT3. The hackathon project was well received in the event, and was awarded second place overall and best deisgn.
